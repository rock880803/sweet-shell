about

小蛤蜊取自諧音「隔離」，但蛤蜊和隔離之間最大的不同是，隔離是將兩者區分開，而蛤蜊卻因為外層有保護殼，具有呵護的意思。
小蛤蜊創建於5/5，並於5/8正式發布，目的就是為了讓所有住宿生都能夠在防疫上得到最大的幫助。

“The Sweet Shell” is named based on the meaning of “shell” describing how it provides a shield of protection to pamper people. It is established on May fifth and published on the eighth. The purpose is to maximize the pandemic prevention assistance provided to the users.

line-bot

透過Line聊天機器人，可以將相同的流程建立成一套程序。
如此一來，每位使用者皆可在第一時間完成手續。
而遇到較複雜的問題，再透過其它服務的同學一對一回覆。

With the help of LINE-BOT, the procedure is established to a program. Therefore, every user is able to finish the procedure at the first time. If there are any complicated questions, we will adopt one-on-one service to users in need.

快篩使用說明書

instruction

  • step

    甩一下試劑管使液體落至管底

    Collect the buffer fluid at the bottom of the test tube by shaking it.

  • step

    撕開封膜

    Peel off the seal.

  • step

    由棉棒包裝上標示”PEEL HERE”的位置打開到一半

    Look for the “PEEL HERE” sign to peel open the swab package halfway.

  • step

    不要碰到棉花棒的頭部

    Do not touch the top of the swab

  • step

    手指握在棉棒的尾端1/3處。

    Hold the swab towards the end of 1/3 point.

  • step

    將棉棒前端1-2公分伸入其中一邊鼻孔，抵著鼻腔壁旋轉5次以上

    Insert the swab 1-2cm into one of the after for nares. Rotate the swabagainst the nasal wall more than 5 times and withdraw.

  • step

    從鼻孔中取出。使用同一棉棒，在另一鼻孔中重複上述步驟

    Repeat the other anterior using the same swab.

  • step

    將棉棒頭放進試劑管液體中，上下移動棉棒10次

    Put the tip of the swab into the test tube and move the swab up and down more than 10 times.

  • step

    邊擠壓管壁編取出棉棒，以擠出棉棒中液體

    Remove the swab while passing against the sides the tube to squeeze the liquid from the swab.

  • step

    將管口緊密地蓋上

    Close the cap tightly.

  • step

    滴三滴液體在試片上標示“x3 ”旁的圓孔中，

    Dispense three drops into the well next to “x3 ”

  • step

    15-20分鐘後讀取結果。(請勿在20分鐘後讀取)

    Read at 15~20 minutes. (Do not read after 20 minutes)
